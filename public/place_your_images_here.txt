You can upload your clinical images (e.g., JPEG, PNG, etc.) directly into this 'public' folder.
You can then reference them in your cases file (/src/data/cases.ts) using absolute paths like:
"/my_image.png" or "/inspection_rash.jpg"

To upload:
1. Drag and drop files from your computer into this 'public' directory in the file explorer.
2. Or use the upload option in your file explorer interface.
